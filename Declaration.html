Declaration of Economic Interdependence

Preamble

When in the course of human events it becomes necessary for the citizens of a community to unite in the pursuit of prosperity, equity, and accountability, a solemn declaration is required to affirm the mutual responsibilities and shared destiny of all who call this city home. In recognition of our collective potential and the challenges we face, we, the citizens of Gary, Indiana, do hereby assert our commitment to the principles of economic interdependence, shared governance, and communal progress.
We hold these truths to be self-evident: that all citizens are endowed with the inalienable rights of dignity, opportunity, and security; that a community is strongest when it acts in the interest of the common good; and that an equitable and accountable government is essential to achieving and sustaining economic renewal and social cohesion.
To ensure the prosperity of present and future generations, we establish this Declaration of Economic Interdependence, committing ourselves to the revitalization and equitable development of our beloved city.

Article I: Principles of Economic Interdependence

Mutual Responsibility

Every citizen has a role in shaping the future of Gary through active participation in economic, social, and civic endeavors.

Shared Prosperity

The wealth and opportunities of the city shall be developed and distributed in ways that promote equity, sustainability, and inclusivity.

Transparent Governance

Public officials and agencies must operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring that all decisions are made in the interest of the people.

Community-Driven Development

Economic development initiatives shall prioritize local input, empowering residents to shape their neighborhoods and benefit directly from revitalization efforts.

Ethical Stewardship

All resources, including public lands, funds, and investments, shall be managed responsibly to maximize benefits for the community.

Article II: The Role of “We the People”

Advocacy and Oversight

The Citizens Advocacy Committee, known as "We the People," shall act as a steward of this Declaration, ensuring adherence to its principles by all governmental and private entities engaged in the city’s economic development.

Public Engagement

The Committee shall organize forums, workshops, and hearings to gather input from residents and ensure their voices are heard in all major decisions affecting the city.

Accountability Mechanisms

Regular reviews of public projects and expenditures shall be conducted, with findings reported transparently to the public.

Conflict Resolution

The Committee shall mediate disputes between citizens, government officials, and private entities, promoting collaboration and equitable outcomes.

Article III: Commitments to Economic Revitalization

Property Utilization

All municipally owned properties shall be managed with the goal of transforming them into vibrant assets that benefit the community and enhance the city’s tax base.

Local Empowerment

Priority shall be given to local residents and businesses in all economic development initiatives, ensuring that the benefits of revitalization remain within the city.

Equitable Opportunity

Programs shall be established to provide training, resources, and support to individuals and businesses, fostering inclusive economic growth.

Sustainability

Development efforts shall align with principles of environmental stewardship, promoting green building practices and renewable energy to create a sustainable future.
